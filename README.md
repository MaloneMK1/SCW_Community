# SCW Community Da Mecca

SCW Community Da Mecca is a web-based companion site for SCW, a long-running custom wrestling universe developed and expanded over many years.

What began as a simple Word document used to track championships, rankings, factions, events, and wrestler histories has evolved into a larger creative project. This repository represents the ongoing effort to transform SCW into a modern web application where fans can explore the history, championships, factions, and stories that make up the SCW universe.

## Vision

The goal of SCW Community Da Mecca is to provide a public-facing wrestling universe website that anyone can browse while giving authorized SCW leadership the tools needed to maintain and expand the world behind the scenes.

Visitors will be able to:

- View championships and title holders
- Explore rankings and wrestler profiles
- Browse events and match cards
- Learn about factions and organizations
- Read rules, history, and community information
- Follow ongoing SCW storylines and developments

Authorized administrators will be able to:

- Sign in securely
- Add, edit, and remove site content
- Manage championships and title histories
- Update rankings and wrestler information
- Create and manage factions
- Publish events and match results
- Maintain rules and community information
- Upload and replace images and logos
- Manage tournaments and standings
- Control site settings, colors, and visual presentation

In short, administrators will be able to manage the content of the SCW universe without directly editing website code.

## Project History

SCW began as a personal wrestling universe tracked through documents and spreadsheets. Over time it expanded into a larger world-building project featuring original wrestlers, factions, championships, events, rivalries, and stories.

The SCW Community Da Mecca website represents the next stage of that journey: preserving the history of SCW while creating tools that allow the universe to continue growing and evolving for years to come.

From a Word document to a living web application, SCW continues to grow alongside the community that created it.
