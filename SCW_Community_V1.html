Community Rules

  • No Spamming: Refrain from spamming strong strikes, repeated moves, or repeated combos.
  • Ground Moves: When your opponent is down, only two ground moves are permitted.
  • No Exploits: Broken moves, tactics, or game exploits may result in disqualification or removal after review.
  • Pins: Pins are only allowed after Signature or Finisher moves.
  • Submissions: Slider submissions are the only permitted submission technique.
  • Arguments: If back-and-forth arguing continues for more than an hour or ten consecutive messages, leadership may book a match to settle it.
  • Banned Moves: Banned and restricted moves may change throughout the season. Request the current banned moves list in league chat.
